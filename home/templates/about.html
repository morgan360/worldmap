An Introduction to OnlyWorlds

OnlyWorlds is a game developer’s approach to world building.

  1. Finding a data framework that supports any type of (non) fictional world setting.
  2. Having this framework support as broad a range of simulation capabilities as possible.

Major steps along the way include an open source Unity World Engine and a master thesis project that investigated the translation of political power dynamics into a general playful data framework. OnlyWorlds is now in closed alpha with two applications available for testing: a mobile app developed in Flutter, and a desktop wrapper for Unity. Initial focus is on validating, expanding, and refining its data structures. If you are interested in contributing to this development, please feel free to leave your email and a message here.
