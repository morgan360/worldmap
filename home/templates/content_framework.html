OnlyWorlds is a platform that maintains, and operates on, an open source data format

This data format aims to represent fictional or historical worlds

Each such world is a snapshot at a specific point in time, and given body through its world Elements

These Elements, of 18 categories, have attributes that can be customized, linked, and operated on as digital objects

So that a world might be constructed in a writing app, sent to a map making tool, then used in games or simulation software
