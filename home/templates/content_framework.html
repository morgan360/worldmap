World building is, at its core, an activity of organizing creative data

There are countless ways to write, organize and display worlds: tools, apps, docs, wikis, websites

Most of these domains have limited options for exporting and transferring worlds effectively to others

OnlyWorlds aims to define a common format for world building

As defined in these three key goals:

1. Provide a data structure 'blueprint' for creating worlds and their content

2. Allow users maximum flexibility and range in building worlds with this blueprint

3. Make world files transferable across tools, games, devices and web

1. Core data structures are open source, free to use, and community-driven

2. Users keep full ownership and control over their world data and visibility

3. Allow any developers and artists to build into the framework, on whatever pricing model

4. Promote collaborative development on the framework, its tools, its worlds

5. The framework should be as flexible and intuitive as possible, for the broadest range of fantasy and history worlds
