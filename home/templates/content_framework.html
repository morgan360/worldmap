Structural Goals

1. Provide a data structure blueprint for capturing worlds and their content

2. Allow building of any fictional / history worlds with blueprint

3. Make world files transferable across tools, games, devices and web

Intent

1. Core data structures are open source, free to use, and community-driven/p>

2. Users keep full ownership and control over their world data and visibility

3. Allow any developers and artists to build into the framework, on whatever pricing model

4. Promote collaborative development on the framework, its tools, its worlds

5. Core OnlyWorlds applications are always free: 1 mobile, 1 desktop, 1 web
