OnlyWorlds facilitates the storage and transfer of worlds on its server.

A prototype system for this is currently in place: worlds can be exported from the Companion or Explorer, and are then coupled to a 10-digit 'world key'.

UI for viewing and managing world files is under construction, and full, authenticated world management is planned for the 1.0 release.

For now: write down your keys, only share these in full confidence, and keep a local copy of your worlds using the Companion's copy to clipboard function.
