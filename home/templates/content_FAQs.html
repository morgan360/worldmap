What does OnlyWorlds do?

  • Offer a data structure to build applications and worlds on top of, tools that work on it, and ways to ship worlds around

How do I use this?

  • Download the mobile app, create some things, export world, import in Explorer, make more edits, send back to mobile, give some feedback, help develop

Most of my fields are locked on mobile?!

  • Attribute groups and fields can be permanently unlocked through the Progression system, but you can longpress a locked group to unlock everything until the app is closed

What's with all the categories and groups and fields and types and subtypes that I totally don't agree with?

  • Consider the current state a starting point that is workable for simulation and game-integration. It needs a lot of user feedback to rework and refine it towards maximum usability while retaining this data flexibility

What are you going to charge for this?

  • I will always keep the framework and essential tools that I develop for it completely free. Other developers are welcome to offer software with any pricing models, and I hope to offer optional services and perhaps a paid app once the core is in place
