Privacy Policy for OnlyWorlds

Welcome to OnlyWorlds, a platform for world creation, transfer, and simulation. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data on our platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the full capabilities of our world building tools and services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register on our website, we require your name and email address. This information is necessary to create your account, verify your identity, and provide you with essential notifications.
  • Content Data: OnlyWorlds facilitates the definition, storage and transfer of OnlyWorld files, which are predominantly text based. The user retains full ownership and control over all the content they create, and can choose to collaborate and publish as they see fit.

2. Use of Your Information

We use your personal information exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To manage your account, provide customer support, and ensure you have access to our services.
  • Communication: We may use your email address to send you important updates about our services or notify you of special events related to the platform. We promise to keep these communications minimal and relevant.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We are committed to not sharing or selling your personal information or created content with any third parties. Your data is used solely for the purposes stated in this policy.

4. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information and content from unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. Our security practices include encrypted storage and secure data transmission.

5. Ownership and Control of Content

You maintain full ownership of the content you create on OnlyWorlds. Our platform does not claim any ownership rights over your creations. We provide the tools and infrastructure to facilitate your creative process, while ensuring that you can freely manage and control the visibility and sharing settings of your content.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about OnlyWorlds or its privacy practices, please contact us at onlyworldsdev@gmail.com.
